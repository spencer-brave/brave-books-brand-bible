Why Lora?

Lora is the sweet spot between "editorial authority" and "warm personality." It has gorgeous italics (critical for those hero headlines where the book title is italicized), enough character in the letterforms to be memorable, and a warmth that Libre Baskerville lacks. It works beautifully at large display sizes AND smaller subheadings. Full variable weight range means no extra font files. It says "curated bookstore" rather than "newspaper" or "law firm."
